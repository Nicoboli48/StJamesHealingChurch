Welcome Here

St. James Healing Church of Christ is a humble and transparent expression of God's grace — a community built on intimacy, accountability, and the transforming power of Christ's healing for all people.

Our purpose is to minister through worship, proclamation, Christian education, and service — carrying out the Great Commission and transforming our city and the nations by offering the healing of Christ to everyone who walks through our doors.

Together, we are committed to listen to and implement whatever plan God has instructed us to carry out — taking the word of God into the world, helping others in our community and in the church, caring for fellow members, upholding and supporting each other, and continuing to learn and spread the tenets of the scriptures. With responsibility to serve, we are all committed to put God's word into action.

— Rev. Aminata Deen, Spiritual Head & Rev. Nicolah Alharazim, Assistant Spiritual Head
