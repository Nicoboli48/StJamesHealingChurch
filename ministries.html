Where Will You Serve?

God has given every member a gift and a calling. Our ministries are designed to help you discover yours — and use it to bless this community and beyond.

Worship Ministry

Led by Rev. Immurandi Vandi & Rev. Aminata Deen

Our Worship Ministry is the heartbeat of St. James. Led by Rev. Immurandi Vandi and anchored by the powerful lead vocals of Pastor Aminata Deen, our choir and worship team usher the congregation into the presence of God every Sunday. From choir rehearsals to sound setup, this ministry serves with excellence and passion.

Healing & Prayer Ministry

Led by Rev. Emilie Pearce-Chapman

At the core of who we are is the belief that God still heals today. Led by Rev. Emilie Pearce-Chapman, this ministry stands in faith for wholeness — spirit, soul, and body. Through dedicated prayer services, intercessory prayer, and healing sessions, we believe no situation is too hard for God.

Prayer Intercession Healing Services Open to All

Joy of Life Inc.

Led by Rev. Yvette Robinson

Joy of Life Inc. is our outreach and community service arm, led by Rev. Yvette Robinson. This ministry extends God's love beyond the walls of our church — serving the community, supporting those in need, and spreading the joy of the Lord through acts of kindness, charity, and Gospel outreach.

Prophetess Ministry

Led by Prophetess Batilie Kanu

The Prophetess Ministry, led by Prophetess Batilie Kanu, operates in the prophetic gifts of the Holy Spirit to bring encouragement, direction, and edification to the body of Christ. This ministry is dedicated to hearing from God and releasing His word with accuracy, love, and humility.
