✦ First Time?

What To Expect

1

A Warm Welcome

From the moment you arrive, our greeters and ushers will make sure you feel at home. No one is a stranger at St. James.

2

Uplifting Worship

Experience powerful, Spirit-filled praise and worship that will lift your spirit and draw you closer to God.

3

Relevant Teaching

Our messages are rooted in Scripture and speak directly to the real challenges of everyday life.

4

Come As You Are

There's no dress code or perfect version of yourself required. Just come as you are — God meets you right where you are.

5

Children Are Welcome

We love families! Children's ministry is available during Sunday service so the whole family can be blessed.

6

Service Length

Sunday worship services typically run about 90 minutes. Come expecting a full, life-changing experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are! Some members dress up, others come casually. We care more about your presence than your outfit.

Do I need to bring a Bible?

Not at all — scriptures are displayed during service. But you're absolutely welcome to bring your own.

Is there childcare available?

Yes! We have children's ministry available during Sunday morning service for kids of all ages.

How do I get connected after my first visit?

Our welcome team will reach out to you after your first visit. You can also fill out a connection card during service or contact us directly.
