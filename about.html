✦ Our Story

About St. James

A family rooted in faith, united in love, and called to bring healing and hope to every life we touch.

✦ Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

St. James Healing Church of Christ is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community dedicated to the worship of God, the healing of the broken, and the empowerment of every believer to walk in their God-given purpose. We are more than a church — we are a family.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ — teaching, equipping, and sending believers to bring healing, hope, and the Gospel to our community and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a house of prayer and healing where every person — regardless of background — encounters the transformative power of God's love and leaves forever changed.

Our Values

Faith, healing, community, integrity, and service. Everything we do flows from these five pillars and our deep commitment to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Our History

St. James Healing Church of Christ was founded on a simple but powerful belief — that God still heals today. What began as a small gathering of faithful believers has grown into a thriving, multi-generational church family.

✦ Our Passion

A Humble & Transparent Expression of God's Grace

To become a humble and transparent expression of God's grace — to live our lives within an intimate and accountable community, and to transform our city and the nations by offering the healing of Christ to all people.

✦ Our Purpose

Carrying Out the Great Commission

To minister through worship, proclamation, Christian education, and service — carrying out the Great Commission as set forth in Matthew 28:19-20, making disciples of all nations.

✦ What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

1

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith and life.

2

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

3

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is a free gift through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works.

4

Divine Healing

We believe God still heals today — spirit, soul, and body — through prayer and faith in His Word.

5

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the operation of spiritual gifts in the church today.

6

The Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead.
